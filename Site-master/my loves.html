My Loves

Here is my first and only car so far. I love it more than life itself. It is a 1997 Honda Prelude which is very rare. When I got it it required alot of body and engine work that my dad and I did over the course of a summer four years ago. It is very clean and the inside looks brand new even though it is 20 years old. A year after it was fixed I got rear ended and the car was totaled. I could not let it go so again my dad and I rebuilt it with a salvaged title. Everything underneath the car was redone to pass inspection and since then I drive it everyday with absolutely no issues. I really enjoy the attention I get from people who have a good eye for JDM vehicles.

My other love is hip hop music. Below is my favorite album by Tyler the Creator. The production and lyrical themes of the album is what makes it special to me.
